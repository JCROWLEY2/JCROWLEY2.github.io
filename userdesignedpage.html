Calculating Max Operating Depth


This calculator is great for calcuating your max operating depth. Standard oxygen mixture is 21% or .21. Anything above that is considered a special blend called Nitrox which requires specialized training and calibration and tanks to fill it up. Often times, most scuba diving gear is rated for both Oxygen and Nitrox blends up to a certain percentage.
